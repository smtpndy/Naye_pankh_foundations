About Us

Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation is one of the biggest student-led NGOs of India with operations extended in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities. We are committed to transforming lives and communities with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all.

Our Mission

Service to mankind is the service to god. We strive to revolutionize society by providing quality education, skill development, and support to underprivileged children and families. Our mission is to empower every individual to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

"If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve!" We envision a society where every individual has access to quality education and opportunities, empowering them to contribute positively to their communities and the nation.

Our Programs

📚

Education Support

We provide scholarships, school supplies, and tutoring assistance to help underprivileged children get quality education and build a strong foundation for their future.

🎯

Skill Development

Training programs in various skills like computer literacy, vocational training, and life skills to empower individuals for employment opportunities.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Special initiatives focused on women's education, health awareness, economic independence, and creating a supportive community for women of all ages.

🤝

Community Service

Regular community service activities including health camps, sanitation drives, environmental initiatives, and disaster relief to support vulnerable populations.
